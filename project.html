AI Class Project

This project examines how artificial intelligence is transforming the U.S. public accounting industry, with a focus on labor markets, firm strategy, and long-run structural change. Using a mix of industry data, academic research, and case studies, the analysis explores how AI tools automate routine work, reshape the CPA pipeline, and create new advisory opportunities. The goal is to connect economic theory with real-world technology adoption and policy debates.
